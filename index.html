Vivienne Westwood

Exclusive to Watch Shop, the Orb Exclusive is a true classic that flawlessly executes the iconic brand that is Vivienne Westwood, a brand spanning almost 50 years. The distinct VW Orb logo, inspired by British royal iconography back in the 1980s, stands prominently in the centre of a silver dial. The classic orb is further emphasised in the form of a charm, an embellishment that makes this model truly unique. Perfectly complimenting the design is a patent leather black strap, imprinted with a crocodile-style pattern.
